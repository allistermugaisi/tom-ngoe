LAWYER TOM NGOE ON THE GLASGOW CLIMATE CHANGE CONFERENCE (UNFCCC COP 26)

Tom Nov 7, 2021

Kenyan based international law expert Tom Ngoe has weighed in on the ongoing high level climate change conference organized by the UK in collaboration with Italy at GLASCOW. On the second day of this high level conference, Lawyer Tom Ngoe presented an opinion dossier that reflected mainly on the previous Paris agreement action plans and how africa can add its voice into this global climate conversation.

Lawyer Tom Ngoe also highlighted the need for governments across the world to actively engage the private sector in mitigating climate adversities. Tom Ngoe through his detailed legal opinion, has encouraged worldly leaders who are meeting at GLASCOW to give a key focus on Cabon pricing.

Lawyer Tom Ngoe has also encouraged African leaders who are attending the said convention to actively participate in the course. Lawyer Ngoe believes that the ongoing COP 26 Presents a perfect opportunity for Africa to regain its bargaining power within the community of nations.

Lawyer Tom Ngoe through his law consulting foundation, has brought on board 47 young African lawyers who he gives free internship opportunities especially on international law, public policy development and high level legal opinions.
